About Our App

Leveraging the power of smartphones, we have made the complaint form a mobile-application so that a citizen should not have to go to the municipal corporation for minor complaints, making the process simpler for both the essential workers and the city residents.
Aggregation of complaints and machine learning helps find “hotspots” so they can be dealt with more urgency.

Cross Platform

Our app is available for both Android and iOS users!

Image Upload

The is also the provision of adding an image to your complaint!

Geo-tagging

User's location helps in resolving the complaint at the earliest possible! It also helps identify clusters!

Let's combat COVID-19

We also have the option of reporting COVID-19 related complaints that will be dealt with utmost urgency. User's can also report people not following COVID-19 norms in the city.

APIs Avaiable

We also provide various APIs, including on that lists all the vaccination centers in Chandigarh along with doses available and the current update of cases in the city. Other APIs help us to deploy our machine learning algorithms.

How The App Works?

Sign-Up

Users can sign up for registering any complaints or grievances that they might face as a tricity resident.

Submit Complaints

Users can upload images and text complaints, which get classified into various categories to make them easy to resolve.

Machine Learning

The text field and the location of the complaints undergo classification and clustering algorithm to provide better insights for the municipal corporation to resolve the issues.

Reporting of COVID related issues

Users can report COVID related issues and get details about vaccine slot availability in Chandigarh, via our APIs. The Smart Citizen app is also aimed at battling COVID, by users reporting any failure in COVID norms not being followed.
